Quick Verdict

draft-mtp delivers 29 t/s gen throughput (104% over baseline) with 28% acceptance — nearly 2x faster than ngram-mod's best builds.

Trade-off: prompt processing drops to 451 pps (vs ~450 pps for ngram-mod), and wall time is higher for large contexts due to MTP overhead during prefill.

For short responses (<300 tokens), ngram-mod on b9049/b9113 is competitive. For long responses, draft-mtp dominates.
